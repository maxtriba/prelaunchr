Broken G String was started in 2015 by Max Triba and Joe Goldberg to bring the sexy playfulness of early rock and roll to guitar accessories. Our strings are ultra-high quality and come in light, medium, and heavy gauges for both standard 6-string electric and acoustic guitars. Subscriptions are also available on 1 and 3 month cycles.
